# Super Coal
Super Coal is a small mod that adds different types of coal from Tiny Coal to Compressed Coal to fit you every smelting need. Along with more torches.  

## Features
- New Coals:  
  - **Items**:
    - **Tiny Coal**: Cooks 1 item per piece of Tiny Coal.
    - **Tiny Charcoal**: Cooks 1 item per piece of Tiny Charcoal.
    - **Nether Coal**: Cooks 16 items per piece of Nether Coal.
    - **Tiny Nether Coal**: Cooks 2 items per piece of Tiny Nether Coal.
    - **Ender Coal**: Cooks 32 items per piece of Ender Coal.
    - **Tiny Ender Coal**: Cooks 4 items per piece of Tiny Ender Coal.
  - **Blocks**:
    - **Compressed Coal Block**: Cooks 720 items per block of Compressed Coal.
    - **Charcoal Block**: Cooks 81 items per block of Charcoal.
    - **Compressed Charcoal Block**: Cooks 720 items per block of Compressed Charcoal.
    - **Nether Coal Block**: Cooks 144 items per block of Nether Coal.
    - **Compressed Nether Coal Block**: Cooks 1440 items per block of Compressed Nether Coal.
    - **Ender Coal Block**: Cooks 256 items per block of Ender Coal.
    - **Compressed Ender Coal Block**: Cooks 2880 items per block of Compressed Ender Coal.
    - **Nether Coal Ore**: A Nether Ore that drops Nether Coal when mined.
    - **End Coal Ore**: An End Ore that drops Ender Coal when mined.
- New Torches:  
  - **Tiny Torch**: Emits light level 7, crafted with a stick and a piece of Tiny Coal or Tiny Charcoal.
  - **Stone Torch**: Emits light level 14, crafted with a piece of Coal or Charcoal and a Stone Rod.
  - **Nether Torch**: Emits light level 15, crafted with a piece of Nether Coal and a Nether Rod.
  - **Tiny Stone Torch**: Emits light level 7, crafted with a piece of Tiny Coal or Tiny Charcoal and a Stone Rod.
  - **Tiny Nether Torch**: Emits light level 15, crafted with a piece of Tiny Nether Coal and a Nether Rod.
